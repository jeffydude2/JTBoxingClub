Before coming to the gym

  1. Make sure to bring your own water bottle, towels, and a set of extra clothes.
  2. Come at least 10-15mins before your session.
  3. Walk to the counter and say that you are here for the free trial.
  4. Inform the gym ahead of time if you'll be late or be cancelling your session.

Trial Class

Within 45-60mins we’ll ensure you get the best results while having fun!

After Class

  1. Our showering facilities are free, feel free to use after session.
  2. Talk to people to learn more about our community.
  3. Walk over to the counter and say that you are here for the free trial they’ll guide you
  4. Inform the gym ahead of time if you’ll be late or be canceling your session.

Programs

Kid Boxing
